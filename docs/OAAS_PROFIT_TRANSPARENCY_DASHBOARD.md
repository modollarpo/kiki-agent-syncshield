## Key Features of This Dashboard

### 1. **Net Profit Focus (Not Gross Revenue)**
The main KPI is **Net Profit Uplift**, not revenue growth. This aligns KIKI's incentives with the client's bottom line.

**Formula shown:**
```
Net Uplift = (New Revenue - Baseline Revenue) - (New Ad Spend - Baseline Ad Spend)
Success Fee = Net Uplift √ó 20%
```

### 2. **Ad Spend Transparency**
Shows exactly how much the client spent on ads (they pay Meta/Google directly) and how KIKI managed that budget.

**Why ad spend increased:**
- SyncFlow identified profitable audiences
- SyncValue predicted high LTV ‚Üí justified higher CPA
- MarginGuardian prevented unprofitable bids

### 3. **ROI Calculator**
Shows the client's return on every dollar paid to KIKI:
```
Your Net Gain = Net Uplift - Success Fee
ROI = Your Net Gain √∑ Success Fee

Example: $32,000 √∑ $8,000 = 4.0x (400% ROI)
```

### 4. **Agent Attribution**
Pie chart showing which AI agents contributed to the net uplift:
- SyncFlow: Ad optimization (45%)
- SyncEngage: Retention flows (30%)
- SyncValue: LTV prediction (15%)
- SyncCreate: Creative generation (10%)

### 5. **Zero-Risk Guarantee Tracker**
Historical log showing months where KIKI charged $0 due to negative net uplift.

Example:
- Oct 2025: -$5k net uplift ‚Üí $0 fee (Zero-Risk active)
- Builds trust: "KIKI only gets paid when you profit"

### 6. **Future Projections**
SyncValue's forecasts for next 3 months, showing expected:
- Net profit uplift
- Success fees
- Client's net gain
